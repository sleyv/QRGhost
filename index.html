FAQ
Quick help and limits for encrypting / decrypting secrets.

What is this?: A secure, plausible-deniability focused data vault. It packs up to 3 secrets into a single QR code using a TrueCrypt-style nested hidden volume architecture.

How Deniability Works: Secrets are encrypted like an onion. The innermost layer (e.g., your Real Seed) is padded with perfect cryptographic noise to exactly fill the QR code. The outer layer (e.g., your Distress Seed) wraps around it. An attacker cannot prove the inner layer exists; it looks identical to random padding.

Encrypt: Provide at least 1 Secret + Password pair. The order matters! Outer secrets wrap inner ones. If you want maximum deniability, fill all 3 inputs.

Decrypt: You can scan a code via camera, upload an image file, or simply paste (Ctrl+V) an image of the QR code directly into this page. Enter your passwords and click Decrypt to peel the onion layers. Important: Because the outer layers wrap the inner layers, you MUST provide the outer passwords to access any inner secrets.

Limits & Truncation: If your text is too big for the QR code, a "water-filling" algorithm will automatically and safely truncate it, ensuring small secrets survive while large secrets are truncated to fit the available space. A warning will appear if data is lost.

Privacy: All encryption (AES-GCM) and key derivation (Argon2id @ 96MiB) occurs locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent to a server.
